Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 1, 2026

The short version: Your memories and conversations are stored on your device. When you send a message, it is processed by a third-party AI service to generate a response, then the response is returned to your device. We collect anonymous usage analytics with no personally identifiable information. We cannot see, access, or read anything you save in Coconut.

1. Who We Are

Coconut is provided by Nathan Benjamin Kallison. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Coconut mobile application ("the App"). If you have questions, contact us at privacy@getcoconut.xyz.

2. Information We Collect

Information stored on your device

The memories, notes, reminders, events, and conversations you create in Coconut are stored locally on your device using Apple's on-device storage. We do not have access to this data. We cannot read, view, or retrieve your stored content.

Information sent to third-party AI services

When you send a message in Coconut, the content of that message (along with recent conversation context) is sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate a response. This is necessary for Coconut to understand your messages and respond intelligently. We do not send any personally identifiable information — such as your name, email address, device ID, or account information — to Anthropic alongside your messages. From Anthropic's perspective, the data it receives is anonymous and cannot be tied to any individual user. Anthropic's API usage policy states that data sent through their API is not used to train their AI models. We encourage you to review Anthropic's privacy policy for details on how they handle data.

Anonymous usage analytics

We use PostHog to collect anonymous usage analytics to understand how people use Coconut and improve the App. This data contains no personally identifiable information. We cannot connect analytics data to individual users or to the content stored in your App. Examples of what we track: app opens, feature usage patterns, and error rates. Examples of what we never track: message content, memory content, names, email addresses, or any personal information.

Information shared via Apple services

If you use the sharing feature in Coconut, shared content is transmitted via Apple's CloudKit infrastructure. This data is governed by Apple's privacy policy and iCloud terms. Subscription purchases are processed by Apple. We receive confirmation of your subscription status but do not receive your payment details.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the limited information we collect to operate and provide the App's core functionality (AI-powered responses), understand aggregate usage patterns to improve Coconut, and manage subscription status. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We do not use your data to train AI models.

4. Data Retention

Your memories and conversations are stored on your device for as long as you keep them. Deleting content in the App removes it from your device. Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data. Anonymous analytics data is retained in aggregate form and cannot be traced back to you.

5. Data Security

Your data is stored on your device using Apple's standard data protection. Messages sent to the AI service are transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS) connections. While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Coconut is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@getcoconut.xyz and we will take steps to delete such information.

7. Your Rights

Because your data is stored locally on your device, you have direct control over it. You can view, edit, or delete any memory or conversation at any time within the App. You can delete all data by uninstalling the App. If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the CCPA. Since we do not collect personally identifiable information, these rights are largely satisfied by default. If you have questions about your rights, contact us at privacy@getcoconut.xyz.

8. Third-Party Services

Coconut uses the following third-party services that may process data as described above:

Anthropic (Claude API) — processes your messages to generate AI responses. Privacy policy.

PostHog — anonymous usage analytics. Privacy policy.

Apple (CloudKit, StoreKit) — sharing features and subscription management. Privacy policy.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the App or by other reasonable means. The "Effective date" at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised. Your continued use of Coconut after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, contact us at privacy@getcoconut.xyz.
